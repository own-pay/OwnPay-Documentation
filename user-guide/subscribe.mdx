## Stay updated

Get notified about new releases, features, and important updates.

<Info>

</Info>

- **Watch** the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/own-pay/OwnPay) to get notified about new releases

- **Join** the [Community Discussions](https://github.com/own-pay/OwnPay/discussions) to engage with other users
